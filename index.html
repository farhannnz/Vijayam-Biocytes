ZinO max – Zinc Oxide Suspension Concentrate (39.5% Zn)

Description: ZinO max is a high-quality Zinc Oxide Suspension Concentrate containing 39.5% elemental Zinc (Zn). It is specially formulated to quickly correct zinc deficiency in crops and improve overall plant growth and yield.

Product Specifications:

  • Composition: Zinc Oxide Suspension Concentrate
  • Zinc Content: 39.5% Zn
  • Application Method: Foliar Spray (suitable for all crops)
  • Recommended Dosage: 120 ml per acre
  • Pack Size: 120 ml

Key Benefits:

  • Rapidly Corrects Zinc Deficiency: Effectively treats zinc deficiency symptoms such as yellowing leaves and stunted growth
  • Enhances Nutrient Uptake (N & P): Improves absorption of essential nutrients like Nitrogen and Phosphorus
  • Promotes Strong Root Development: Encourages deeper and healthier root systems for better nutrient absorption
  • Boosts Flowering & Fruiting: Supports improved flower formation and fruit development
  • Improves Photosynthesis: Maintains healthy green leaves and enhances energy production in plants
  • Reduces Biotic & Abiotic Stress: Helps crops tolerate pest attacks, diseases, and environmental stress
  • Increases Yield & Crop Quality: Leads to higher productivity and better market-quality produce

Suitable Crops:

  • Cereals (Wheat, Rice, Maize)
  • Pulses
  • Oilseeds
  • Fruits & Vegetables
  • Cotton
  • Plantation Crops
  • Horticultural Crops

Application Instructions:

  • Mix 120 ml per acre in the recommended quantity of water
  • Spray evenly on crop foliage
  • Apply during the early morning or late evening for best results
  • Avoid spraying during extreme heat or rainfall

Kusum - Boron Ethanolamine

Description: Kusum is a concentrated liquid boron fertilizer made with ethanolamine to help plants absorb boron better. Boron is important for plant growth, as it supports cell wall development, metabolism, and overall plant health.

Composition:

  • Boron Ethanolamine as Boron: 10%

Benefits:

  • Supports cell wall development and metabolism
  • Helps in flowering and fruiting
  • Improves the use of other fertilizers by boosting nutrient uptake
  • Helps maintain hormonal balance for better plant development and yield
  • Enhances overall plant health

Recommended Crops: Beans, Pulses, Chilli, Tomato, Cotton, Paddy, Redgram, and all Vegetable crops

Pack Size: 120 ml

Instagrow - Liquid Fermented Organic Manure

Description: Instagrow is a fermented liquid organic fertilizer created from organic liquid material, activated by Mountain Microorganisms like yeast, fungi, and bacteria using advanced fermentation technology.

Composition:

  • Total Organic Carbon: 14%
  • Total N, P₂O₅, and K₂O Nutrients: 1.2%
  • Total Moisture Content: 90-97%

Benefits:

  • Provides plant-essential nutrients
  • Rich in vitamins and organic acids
  • Contains plant growth-promoting compounds
  • Improves soil health and microbial activity
  • Promotes overall plant growth and development

Dosage: 2.5 to 3 ml in 1 Liter of water

Recommended Crops: Paddy, Chilli, Tomato, Cotton, Redgram, and all Vegetable crops

Pack Size: 120 ml
