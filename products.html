Description: ZinO max is a high-quality Zinc Oxide Suspension Concentrate containing 39.5% elemental Zinc (Zn). It is specially formulated to quickly correct zinc deficiency in crops and improve overall plant growth and yield.

Product Specifications:

  • Composition: Zinc Oxide Suspension Concentrate
  • Zinc Content: 39.5% Zn
  • Application Method: Foliar Spray (suitable for all crops)
  • Recommended Dosage: 120 ml per acre
  • Pack Size: 120 ml

Key Benefits:

  • Rapidly Corrects Zinc Deficiency: Effectively treats zinc deficiency symptoms such as yellowing leaves and stunted growth
  • Enhances Nutrient Uptake (N & P): Improves absorption of essential nutrients like Nitrogen and Phosphorus
  • Promotes Strong Root Development: Encourages deeper and healthier root systems
  • Boosts Flowering & Fruiting: Supports improved flower formation and fruit development
  • Improves Photosynthesis: Maintains healthy green leaves and enhances energy production
  • Reduces Biotic & Abiotic Stress: Helps crops tolerate pest attacks, diseases, and environmental stress
  • Increases Yield & Crop Quality: Leads to higher productivity and better market-quality produce

BORAIN – Boron 20% (Di Sodium Octaborate Tetrahydrate)

Manufacturer: Vijayam Biocytes Pvt. Ltd.

Description: BORAIN is a premium granular boron micronutrient fertilizer containing 20% Boron derived from Di Sodium Octaborate Tetrahydrate. Boron is one of the most critical micronutrients for plant reproductive growth — it directly influences pollen germination, cell wall formation, sugar transport, and fruit setting. BORAIN ensures rapid correction of boron deficiency and is suitable for all crops.

Composition:

  • Active Ingredient: Di Sodium Octaborate Tetrahydrate
  • Boron (B) Content: 20%
  • Form: Granular / Powder

Pack Size: 250 gm  |  Use: For Agriculture Use Only / केवल कृषि उपयोग के लिए

Key Benefits:

  • Corrects Boron Deficiency: Rapidly fixes boron-deficient conditions that cause hollow stem, cracked fruits, and tip burn
  • Improves Pollination & Fruit Set: Boron is essential for pollen tube growth and successful fertilization
  • Strengthens Cell Walls: Supports structural integrity and rigidity of plant cells
  • Enhances Sugar Transport: Facilitates the movement of sugars from leaves to fruits and roots
  • Boosts Flowering: Promotes abundant and uniform flower formation
  • Improves Crop Quality & Shelf Life: Produces firmer, better-coloured fruits with longer post-harvest life
  • Increases Yield: Balanced boron nutrition leads to higher productivity across all crop types

Suitable Crops:

  • Fruits: Grapes, Banana, Watermelon, Apple, Mango, Pomegranate
  • Vegetables: Tomato, Chilli, Brinjal, Cauliflower, Cabbage
  • Cash Crops: Cotton, Sugarcane, Groundnut
  • Cereals & Pulses: Paddy, Wheat, Soybean, Redgram
  • Flowers & Horticultural Crops

Dosage & Application:

  • Foliar Spray: Dissolve 1–2 gm per liter of water and spray evenly on foliage
  • Soil Application: Mix 2–3 kg per acre with sand or fertilizer and apply uniformly
  • Best applied at flowering and early fruit development stages
