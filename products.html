Protein Hydrolysate Powder (100% Pure)

Description: A mixture of amino acids extracted from natural sources through comprehensive hydrolysis, filtration, pH adjustments, purification, and de-colourization processes. Enhances productivity of all crops through foliar spray and drenching application.

Composition:

  • Total Dissolved Solids: 40-42%
  • pH: 3.5-4.5
  • Total Organic Carbon: 6-7%
  • Total Nitrogen: 6.5-7.0%

Usage: Dissolve 5-6 ml in 1 liter of water and apply through spraying or drenching at initial growth, flowering, and fruiting stages.

Benefits:

  • Enhances chlorophyll synthesis
  • Makes plants stress resistant
  • Promotes flowering & fruit formation
  • Helps promoting soil micro flora
  • Maintains and improves protein synthesis
  • Enhances photosynthesis making plant lush green

Compatibility: Compatible with all types of insecticides, herbicides, and fungicides.

NATCA Powder (99% Pure)

Description: N-Acetyl Thiazolidine-4-Carboxylic Acid, a plant growth promoter derived from sulphur-rich amino acid L-Cysteine. Serves as an effective plant growth regulator.

Manufacturing Process: L-Cysteine hydrochloride monohydrate is reacted with formaldehyde solution, alkalized using caustic alkali, subjected to acetylation with acetic anhydride, pH adjusted, centrifuged, washed, dried, and packed.

Composition: 99% Pure NATCA

Usage: Dissolve at 500-1000 ppm and apply through foliar spray or drenching at critical growth stages.

Benefits:

  • Enhances protein synthesis
  • Controls stress and promotes fast recovery
  • Enhances photosynthesis
  • Supports pollination and fruit formation
  • Improves soil micro flora
  • Enhances plant's hydric balance
  • Increases resistance to unfavorable conditions
  • Supports disease suppression

Application Stages: Initial growth, flowering, and fruiting stages
