 Brief manufacturing procedure of NATCA: 
 L-Cysteine hydrochloride monohydrate is reacted with a formaldehyde solution. The resulting solution is 
alkalized using a caustic alkali solution and subjected to acetylation with acetic anhydride and caustic 
alkali. The acetylated product undergoes pH adjustment using hydrochloric acid. The precipitated 
product is centrifuged, washed to remove excess chloride, dried, and packed for commercial application.

4-Carboxylic Acid Amino acid (NATCA). 
Amino acids play a crucial role in agriculture as they are essential building blocks for proteins, which are 
vital for plant growth and development. In agriculture, the concept of amino acids is closely tied to their 
role as organic molecules that contribute to various physiological and biochemical processes in plants.
Whereas N-Acetyl Thiazolidine-4-Carboxylic Acid (NATCA), plant growth promotor and an active 
ingredient derived from the Sulphur-rich amino acid L-Cysteine, serves as an effective plant growth 
regulator. These regulators, often termed phytochromes, are natural chemicals strategically applied to 
distinct plant parts—roots, shoots, leaves, and fruits—to modulate various physiological, chemical and 
biological processes.

About Us:
Vijayam Biocytes Private Limited (VBCPL), established in 2003, specializes in the manufacturing of 
plant booster such as protein hydrolysate (Amino Acid Mixture), N Acetyl Thiazolidine 4-Carboxylic Acid 
Amino acid (NATCA) and their combination products to improve the agricultural productivity and soil 
micro -flora. Our manufacturing facility is situated in Kakalur, Tiruvallur, Tamilnadu. 
In October 2013, the company underwent reconstitution, welcoming additional directors, including Mr. 
Madupalli Mohan Gupta, Mr. Nagarajan Kailasam, Mrs. Padmini Nagarajan, Mr. Madupalli Krishna 
Kishore, and Mr. Madupalli Vijay Kumar. This strategic move aimed to facilitate expansion under the 
Companies Act, 1956. The company Headquartered at Sydenhams Road, Choolai, Chennai-600112,". 
Due to our extensive field experience and the growing demand from farmers, we have recognized 
significant business potential in NATCA and Amino Acids, prompting the decision to expand our 
capacity. As part of this initiative, we acquired land at APIIC Industrial Estate, Renigunta, Andhra 
Pradesh, Tirupathi District, Andhra Predesh and started the production of above-mentioned products 
from 2020-21. Exclusively focusing on Amino Acids and NATCA, we offer a diverse product range, 
including various combination products based on Amino Acid and Hydrolyzed Protein, Plant Growth 
Promoters, and many more. Our products have gained acclaim nationwide, and we prioritize safe 
packaging using hygienic and high-quality materials. 
At Vijayam Biocytes Private Limited, our operations are meticulously conducted by experts with the 
primary goal of achieving set objectives, ultimately ensuring maximum customer satisfaction.
